Digital Competences for Clinical Psychologists

Introduction

These Digital Competences were prepared by an expert reference group of the Group of Trainers in Clinical Psychology, Division of Clinical Psychology, British Psychological Society.

This work was initiated and chaired by Dr Helen Pote and the expert reference group drew together UK academics, clinical psychology practitioners and trainers, services users, digital mental health companies and commissioners all with experience in digital mental health.

Ethical Statement

In using these digital competences and working within the digital sphere with clients and other professionals it is important that Clinical Psychologists adhere to the usual professional and ethical guidelines that guide their practice. Particularly when working within a digital realm Clinical Psychologists should pay attention to issues of client consent and participation, equity of access and choice.
